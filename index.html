Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our tours and services.

You can book a tour directly through our website by selecting your desired tour, choosing a date, and completing the secure online payment. You'll receive instant confirmation via email with all the details.

We offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before your tour starts. For cancellations within 24 hours, a 50% fee applies. Please contact our support team for any changes or cancellations.

Most of our tours are family-friendly and suitable for children. However, some tours involve extensive walking or specific content that may be more appropriate for older children. Please check the tour details or contact us for recommendations.

Yes! We offer private tours for individuals, families, and small groups. Private tours can be customized to your interests and schedule. Contact us for pricing and availability.

We offer tours in English, Italian, Spanish, French, German, and several other languages. Language availability varies by tour, so please check the specific tour page or contact us to confirm.

We recommend comfortable walking shoes, water, sunscreen, and a hat for outdoor tours. For church and museum visits, modest clothing covering shoulders and knees is required. Your confirmation email will include specific requirements for your tour.
